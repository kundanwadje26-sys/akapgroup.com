About AKap Group

A story of vision, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to building industries that matter.

Our Foundation

Built on Purpose, Driven by Ambition

AKap Group was founded with a singular vision — to create a diversified industrial enterprise that delivers lasting value to customers, partners, and communities across India.

From our earliest days, we have believed that great businesses are built on three pillars: exceptional people, rigorous processes, and an unyielding commitment to quality. These principles have guided every decision, every partnership, and every venture we have undertaken.

Today, AKap Group stands as a testament to what is possible when vision meets execution — spanning multiple industries, serving diverse markets, and creating opportunities at every level of our ecosystem.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

Every great enterprise is shaped by the choices made at each crossroads. Here are the moments that have defined AKap Group.

2024 — Foundation

AKap Group is Established

Founded by Mr. Ashish Ashok Wadje and Mr. Kundan Ashok Wadje with a vision to build a trusted, diversified industrial enterprise committed to quality and innovation.

2024 — Expansion

First Business Verticals Launched

AKap Group launches its first core business divisions, establishing foundational operations and building early partner relationships across Maharashtra.

2025 — Network Growth

Pan-India Partner Network

Expanding our Retailer, Dealer, Builder, and Plumber network across multiple states, with a structured coding and management system introduced to ensure accountability and tracking.

2026 — Digital Transformation

Digital Infrastructure & Portal Launch

Investing in digital platforms, including the Retailer Partner Portal, to streamline operations, improve partner experience, and enable data-driven decision-making.

Future

The Road Ahead

Continuing to scale across sectors, deepen market presence, and build industry-leading businesses that create enduring value for all stakeholders.
