Leadership at AKap Group

Guided by experience, driven by vision, and committed to creating enduring value for every stakeholder in our ecosystem.

Mr. Ashish Ashok Wadje
Chairman & Managing Director
"Visionary leadership driving innovation, growth and long-term value creation."

Mr. Ashish Ashok Wadje is the Chairman and Managing Director of AKap Group, the principal architect of the group's strategic direction and long-term vision. His leadership style combines bold ambition with operational discipline — a combination that has proven essential in building a diversified enterprise from the ground up.

Under his stewardship, AKap Group has established itself across multiple business verticals, with a commitment to quality and innovation that permeates every level of the organization. He is deeply involved in setting the group's priorities, identifying new opportunities for growth, and ensuring that AKap's values of integrity and excellence remain at the core of every business decision.

Mr. Kundan Ashok Wadje
Director & Chief Strategist
"The strategic mind behind growth, diversification, and long-term business excellence."

Mr. Kundan Ashok Wadje serves as Director and Chief Strategist of AKap Group, responsible for the group's strategic planning, business diversification initiatives, and long-term growth agenda. He brings a rigorous analytical approach to identifying market opportunities and translating them into actionable business plans.

As the strategic mind of the group, Mr. Kundan oversees the development of AKap's partner network, manages inter-business synergies, and leads the group's efforts to build scalable, future-ready operations. His focus on systems, process excellence, and data-driven decision-making is central to the group's ability to operate across multiple verticals with clarity and consistency.
